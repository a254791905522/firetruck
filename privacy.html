Fire Truck Rescue Sim Games

Effective and last updated: March 2026

Our commitment

Fire Truck Rescue Sim Games is an action and simulation game where you drive fire trucks and complete rescue missions. We are committed to transparency about how we handle information in connection with the game and this website. This document describes our privacy practices for the Fire Truck Rescue Sim Games app and related services.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, or physical address. We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP addresses for the purpose of identifying you. We do not collect precise location or geolocation data. Gameplay data (scores, levels, session duration) is not collected or sent to our servers.

Local device storage

Progress, settings, and in-app preferences (for example, sound toggles or unlocked trucks and levels) may be stored locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us or to third parties. You can delete this data by uninstalling the app.

Third parties and links

We do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising networks that collect your personal data. This site may link to external destinations (such as the App Store). Those destinations have their own privacy policies, which we do not control. We encourage you to read their policies when you leave our site.

Children and age

Fire Truck Rescue Sim Games may be used by players of various ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Our approach is consistent with applicable privacy laws, including those that protect children’s information where relevant.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy document from time to time. The date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the game or this website after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We recommend checking this page periodically.
